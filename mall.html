FEATURES

of the Ckj's Antique Mall

Climate Controlled

Rain or Shine, we have you covered. With climate controlled aisles you are in a comfortable environment any time of the year.

Pet Friendly

Bring your furry friends, no need to leave them at home, hotel or in the car.

Shipping Service

We also offer shipping and local delivery as well as gift certificates. See our friendly and knowledgeable staff for details.

Handicap Accessible

Wide aisles and entrance doors allow for easy accessibility and maneuvering. 

Call Buttons for Assistance

Call buttons for assistance are located on every aisle, allowing our sales staff to take your finds and treasures to the front desk, so you don’t have to carry them around the mall.

Numbered Checkout System

When you’re ready, our take-a-number system makes checkout fast and easy.

Managed by Dealers

Our knowledgeable sales staff, all of whom are antiques dealers with their own spaces in the mall, are on hand to assist you during your visit.
